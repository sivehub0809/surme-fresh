Privacy

SurMe Data Principles

SurMe is built on trust. We do not sell user data. Chats and account data are protected with row-level security, and sensitive integration data is handled only so the assistant can work.

Data may be shared with trusted AI model providers and integrations, including OpenAI, Google Gemini, Google, and Telegram, strictly for assistant functionality.

Users can disconnect integrations or delete their account data from Settings. The product should always make these controls visible and understandable.
